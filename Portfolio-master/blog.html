"This is my journey in web development."

My background is in the wellness industry. I became a Licensed Massage Therapist in 2005. I am a natural healer and Reiki master. One might ask, "How did I get into web development?" Ok, let me tell you. I injured my shoulder really bad in the summer of 2017 and I thought my wellness career was finished. I began looking for other career avenues and education without having to go to a university. I lacked resources such as money and time to spend my whole day in a formal school setting. What will I do? I took computer courses thoughtout my school days and I new some web developers that said I could learn how to code from the comfort of my home or coffee shop. Wow, that sounds legit. So I began my journey. In Sept. of 2017 I started phase 1 of the scholarship challange with Udacity. This was my first experience with HTML. They give you all of the tools and course work for free HTML and CSS. I thought it was wonderful and I was using my brain in different ways. In Jan. 2018 I learned that I was accepeted into phase 2 and was completely floored because there are some really seasoned coders out there. So I began my challage with list of projects and we had a three month window to get the course work completed. Well "I DID IT". At the end of April 2018 I found out that I was fortunate to recieve the Grow with Google Scholarship. I could not believe it!! What an amazing oppurtuntiy I have recieved. Thank you Udacity and Google for changing my life!! Now between running my own business and raising kids I am doing this. At times I want to bang my head against a wall, but I keep moving forward. I have two projects left before I can get my Nanodegree and I could not be more happy or proud to be exactly where the universe wants me to be. Never give up on your dreams. Take one day at a time. Remember, You are worth it and you derserve the best.

That is a wrap!

When you think you can't do this keep moving forward and you will get it.
